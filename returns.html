Overview

At Hussain Healthcare, your satisfaction is our commitment. We understand that sometimes a product may not meet your expectations or may arrive in an unsatisfactory condition. Our Returns & Refunds Policy is designed to make the process as straightforward and fair as possible.

This policy applies to all purchases made through our website, mobile app, and in-store at our Karachi location. By placing an order with us, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

✅

Our Promise: We aim to resolve all return and refund requests within 5–7 business days. Your trust in us matters, and we will always work to make it right.

2

Return Eligibility

To qualify for a return or refund, your item must meet the following conditions:

✅ Eligible for Return

  • Item returned within 7 days of delivery date
  • Product is unused and in original condition
  • Original packaging, labels, and seals are intact
  • Receipt or proof of purchase is available
  • Item is not a prescription medicine
  • Product defect or damage reported within 48 hours
  • Wrong item was delivered

❌ Not Eligible for Return

  • Return request made after 7 days of delivery
  • Item has been opened, used, or consumed
  • Packaging, seals, or labels have been removed or damaged
  • Prescription medicines of any kind
  • Personal hygiene or intimate wellness products once opened
  • Items purchased during a clearance or final sale
  • Perishable items (temperature-sensitive products)
ℹ️

The 7-day return window begins from the date of delivery confirmation, not the order date. Please inspect your items as soon as they arrive.

3

Non-Returnable Items

Due to health, safety, and regulatory requirements, the following categories of products cannot be returned or refunded under any circumstances, except in cases of manufacturing defects:

⚠️

Prescription Medicines: All prescription medications are strictly non-returnable in compliance with Pakistan's Drug Regulatory Authority of Pakistan (DRAP) regulations. This is to ensure patient safety and prevent tampering.

The following additional product types are non-returnable once opened or used:

💊

Prescription & Controlled Medicines

All medications requiring a valid prescription, including antibiotics, controlled substances, and chronic disease medications.

🩸

Diagnostic & Testing Supplies

Blood glucose test strips, pregnancy tests, COVID test kits, and other diagnostic consumables once packaging is opened.

🌸

Personal & Intimate Wellness

Sexual wellness products, intimate hygiene items, and similar personal care products once opened.

🍼

Infant Formula & Nutritional Supplements

Baby formulas, medical nutrition products, and supplements that have been opened or seal is broken.

❄️

Cold-Chain / Temperature-Sensitive Products

Insulin, vaccines, biologics, and any product requiring refrigeration cannot be returned due to storage integrity concerns.

4

How to Initiate a Return

Returning an item is simple. Follow these steps to initiate your return request:

1

Contact Our Team

Call us at +92 331 111 3292 or email returns@hussainhealthcare.biz within 7 days of delivery. Provide your order number and reason for return.

2

Receive Return Authorisation

Our team will review your request and issue a Return Authorisation (RA) number within 24–48 hours if your return is approved.

3

Pack the Item Securely

Place the item in its original packaging with all accessories, inserts, and documentation. Write the RA number clearly on the outside of the package.

4

Ship or Drop Off

Send the package to our store at Block 4, Gulshan-e-Iqbal, Karachi or schedule a pickup with our team (available within Karachi).

5

Inspection & Confirmation

Once received, our pharmacy team will inspect the item within 1–2 business days and notify you of the outcome via SMS or call.

📸

Tip: Take photos of the item and packaging before shipping it back. This protects you in case of any disputes during the inspection process.

5

Refund Process

Once your return has been received and inspected, we will process your refund through the original payment method used at checkout.

Return Received

Item arrives at our pharmacy and is logged in our system.

Day 1–2: Quality Inspection

Our pharmacist team inspects the returned item to confirm it meets return eligibility criteria.

Day 2–3: Refund Approved & Initiated

You receive an SMS/call confirming the refund has been approved and initiated.

Day 5–7: Refund Credited

Refund appears in your bank account, mobile wallet (Easypaisa / JazzCash), or as store credit — depending on original payment method.

Refund methods available:

💳

Bank Transfer / Card Refund: 5–7 business days
Easypaisa / JazzCash: 1–3 business days
Store Credit / Voucher: Instant (valid for 6 months)
Cash Refund (in-store): Same day on drop-off returns

⚠️

Delivery charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error (wrong item sent, damaged in transit). Original shipping costs will be deducted from the refund amount where applicable.

6

Exchanges

We offer product exchanges for eligible items within the 7-day return window. Exchanges are subject to product availability.

To exchange an item, follow the same process as a return (Section 4) and specify in your initial contact that you prefer an exchange. Our team will confirm stock availability before processing.

🔄

If the replacement item is of higher value, you will be asked to pay the price difference. If it is of lower value, the difference will be refunded to you via your original payment method.

7

Damaged or Wrong Items

If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect item, we sincerely apologise. Please follow these steps immediately:

📸

Document the Issue

Photograph the item, the packaging, and the delivery label within 48 hours of receiving the order.

📞

Report to Us Immediately

Call +92 331 111 3292 or WhatsApp us with the photos and your order number. Do not use, open further, or dispose of the item.

✅

We'll Make It Right

We will arrange a free pickup and ship a replacement at no cost, or issue a full refund including original delivery charges — your choice.

⏰

Important: Damage or incorrect item claims must be reported within 48 hours of delivery. Claims made after this window may not be eligible for a free replacement or full refund.

8

Return Shipping Costs

Who pays for return shipping depends on the reason for the return:

✅ We Cover Shipping

  • Wrong item was sent to you
  • Item arrived damaged or defective
  • Manufacturing fault confirmed by pharmacist
  • Order was lost and found delivered incorrectly

📦 You Cover Shipping

  • Change of mind returns
  • Ordered wrong variant or quantity
  • Product not needed anymore
  • Any other reason not due to our error
📍

Customers within Karachi can drop off returns directly at our store: Block 4, Gulshan-e-Iqbal, Karachi — Monday to Sunday, 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM. No shipping cost applies for in-store drop-offs.
